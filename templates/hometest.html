Welcome!

Depression can be a frustrating thing to deal with. But remember that you are not alone. We are here to help you out. Having a way to share your struggle and get suggestions is really important. That's why we have created an anonymous platform where you can take a test by answering few questions.

The data from questionnaire is fed to a highly accurate machine learning model to detect level of depression. Since it uses machine learning, our results are generalized and is not hard coded. This allows us to consider many factors into account to decide upon the level of depression.

The response of the questionnaire will be recorded to further enhance the model and research into the model behavior. Do note that we do not take any personally identifiable information in any form such as cookies. This is really important to ensure privacy and anonymity. It also allows the users to answer questions more freely.

Based on the latest data we have, global suicide rates have been high the past few years. Mental health and depression are yet to be taken seriously by many, as it takes more lives everyday. This project is made in an effort to reduce suicide rates and help people with depression in the IT Industry using a highly accurate machine learning model. We know that the best way to know is to ask. Hence we designed a 15 question questionnaire whose data will be fed into a machine learning model to predict level of depression with 96% accuracy.
